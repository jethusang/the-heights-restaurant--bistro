Dining Excellence at New Heights

Dining Excellence at New Heights Located in the heart of Kimberley, The Heights Restaurant & Bistro offers an unparalleled dining experience combining exceptional cuisine, stunning rooftop views, and warm hospitality.

Our Story

The Heights has established itself as Kimberley's premier dining destination, where culinary artistry meets breathtaking views. Our rooftop location provides the perfect backdrop for memorable dining experiences, whether you're joining us for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

We pride ourselves on our smart casual atmosphere that welcomes everyone, from intimate dinners to business meetings and special celebrations. Our community engagement through social media and events has made us a beloved part of the Kimberley dining scene.
